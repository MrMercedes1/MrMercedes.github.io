Christmas Section

Christmas, a Christian holiday honoring the birth of Jesus, has evolved into a worldwide religious and secular celebration, incorporating many pre-Christian and pagan traditions into the festivities. Christmas is celebrated by many Christians on December 25.It is one of the most important days of the year for Christians, along with Easter when the death and resurrection of Jesus are celebrated.in addition, Modern traditions of Christmas often focus on the giving of gifts.
